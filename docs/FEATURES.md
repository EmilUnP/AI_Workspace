# Features (Current Baseline)

## Platform Direction

- ERP-first product
- SaaS product removed
- Student portal UI removed

## Role Experience

- Platform Owner: global settings, organizations, users, visibility controls
- School Admin: school users, classes, reports
- Teacher: classes, teaching studio, lessons, exams, AI tutor, reports
- Student: data can exist, but direct portal access is blocked

## AI Capabilities

- AI lesson generation
- AI exam generation
- AI course and education-plan assistance
- Teacher AI tutor/chat workflows

## Product Constraints

- Keep student records for admin/teacher operations
- Route denied student sign-ins to `/auth/access-denied`
